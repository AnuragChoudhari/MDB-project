Best in class specifications


Structure:

-R.R.C Frame structure of best in class quality material


Brick work and Plaster:

-ACC Autoclaved Aerated block or burnt bricks or fly ash brickwork for external wall
-Internal walls gypsum finish/cement neeru plaster
-Double coat sand faced cement plaster for external walls


Plumbing & Sanitary Ware:

-Concealed Plumbing
-Sanitary ware-Cera/Jahuar/Hindwave or equivalent fitting in bathroom,toilet and kitchen
-Mixer unit with shower in toilet and bathroom
-Provision for boiler/Geyser
-Provision of Dish Washer machine in Utility/ Dry Balcony


Tile Work:

-Best inclass vitrified flooring in rooms
-4'x 2' living, dining & kitchen
-2'x 2' bed & passages
-Anti- skid ceramic flooring in attached toilet, bathroom, terrace & utility
-7' height dado in toilet and bathroom
-Kitchen Dado tile of height 7'
-Dado tile upto sill level in utility
-Tandoor stone / tile for steps & tile for riser
-Granite kitchen platform with stainless steel sink





Window , Grills & Railings:

-3 track aluminium sliding windows with mosquito net
-Marble / granite for window sills
-MS Grill for protection
-Glass railing for attached terrace/balcony of flat doors


Door,Door Frames & Fixtures:

-Decorative main door with wooden frame
-Good quality FRP/flush waterproof doors for toilets and bathrooms
-Marbel/granite door frames for toilets and bathrooms
-Glass quality door fitting
-Cylindrical locks for bedroom doors


Electrication:

-Best in class modular switches
-Adequate concealed electrical point in all rooms
-ISI marked wiring of polycab/vinay or equivalent brand
-Electric points for refrigerator , oven, exhaust fan & water purifier in kitchen


Painting:

-Internal oil bound distemper point in the entire flat and common area
-External two coat apex or equivalent
-Oil paint to all doors railing and grills(except main door)
